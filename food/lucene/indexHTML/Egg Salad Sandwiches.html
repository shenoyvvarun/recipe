Egg Salad Sandwiches

Lunch/snacks,Egg,Eggs/dairy,Kid Friendly,Brown bag,Taste/mood,To go...,Refrigerator,Stove top,Time to make,Equipment,Presentation
Ingredients
  • eggs, hard cooked, cooled and diced
  • butter, melted
  • Miracle Whip
  • salt
  • Lawry's Seasoned Salt
  • garlic salt
  • celery salt
  • dill weed
  • bread
  • onions, chopped
  • celery, chopped
  • pickle relish
  • green olives
Method
  1. Mix together the eggs, butter, miracle whip and seasonings.
  2. Refrigerate until your ready to make your sandwiches.
  3. Spread on one bread slice then top with another piece of bread.
  4. If you'd like here are a few other ingredients you could add.
  5. Mrs. Peterson could have added the onions and celery, I don't remember.
  6. I know she did not use the relish or olives.
  7. But I thought they may offer a different taste, as I saw them in another recipe.
