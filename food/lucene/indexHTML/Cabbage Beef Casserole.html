Cabbage Beef Casserole

One Dish meal,Rice,Vegetable,Meat,Pasta, rice and grains,Low cholesterol,Healthy 2,Low in...,Weeknight,Oven,Time to make,Equipment
Ingredients
  • cabbage
  • ground beef
  • onion (cut up)
  • green pepper (chopped)
  • cooking oil
  • salt
  • uncooked rice
  • condensed tomato soup
  • tomato sauce
  • water
Method
  1. Brown ground beef in oil; add onion,green pepper, salt and rice.
  2. Slice cabbage in bottom of casserole dish; spread beef mixture over cabbage.
  3. Mix tomato soup and water; add tomato sauce.
  4. Pour over cabbage and meat.
  5. Cover and bake at 350Â° for 1 1/2 hours.
