Italian Sausage, Tomato, and Cream With Bow Ties

Pork,Meat,Pasta, rice and grains,European,Time to make
Ingredients
  • olive oil
  • sweet Italian sausage, casings removed and crumbled
  • dried red pepper flakes
  • diced onion
  • garlic cloves, minced
  • Italian plum tomatoes, coarsely chopped
  • black olives, sliced
  • whipping cream
  • salt
  • bow tie pasta
  • minced fresh parsley
Method
  1. Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add sausage and pepper flakes. Cook until sausage is no longer pink, stirring frequently, about 7 minutes.
  2. Add onion and garlic to skillet and cook until onion is tender and sausage is light brown, stirring occasionally, about 7 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes, olives, cream, salt and parsley. Simmer until mixture thickens slightly, about 4 minutes.
  4. Cook pasta in large pot of boiling salted water until just tender but still firm to bite, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  5. Toss with mixture and sprinkle with parmesan.
