Italian Sausage and Tortellini Soup

Ravioli/tortellini,Pork,Vegetable,Meat,Pasta, rice and grains,European,Low cholesterol,Healthy 2,Low in...,Potluck,Winter,Weeknight,Seasonal,Taste/mood,To go...,Stove top,Time to make,Equipment
Ingredients
  • Italian sausage, casings removed
  • chopped onion
  • garlic cloves, minced
  • beef broth
  • chopped tomatoes
  • tomato sauce
  • zucchini (chopped or sliced)
  • carrot, sliced
  • green bell pepper, chopped
  • dry red wine
  • dried basil
  • dried oregano
  • cheese tortellini
Method
  1. In a large saucepan, saute and crumble Italian sausage until no longer pink.
  2. Drain sausage well on paper towels and pour off all but 1 tablespoon of drippings from saucepan.
  3. Add onion and garlic to saucepan.
  4. Saute until translucent.
  5. Return sausage to pan; add beef broth, tomatoes, tomato sauce, zucchini, carrot, bell pepper, wine, basil, and oregano.
  6. Simmer until vegetables are tender, 40-60 minutes.
  7. Add tortellini and cook until tender.
  8. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  9. Ladle into individual soup bowls and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
