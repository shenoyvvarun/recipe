Marinated Chickpea and Artichoke Salad with Feta

Chickpeas/garbanzos,Beans,Vegetable,Southwest asia (middle east),African,Asian,Low cholesterol,Healthy 2,Low in...,To go...,Refrigerator,Served cold,Time to make,Equipment,Presentation
Ingredients
  • chickpeas, rinsed and drained (garbanzo beans)
  • artichoke hearts, drained
  • roasted sweet red peppers, drained and diced
  • pitted kalamata olive
  • crumbled feta cheese
  • scallions, finely sliced
  • chopped fresh parsley
  • red wine vinegar
  • chopped fresh mint
  • za'atar spice mix (optional)
  • sugar (or to taste)
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • extra virgin olive oil
Method
  1. Place chickpeas, artichoke hearts, red peppers, olives, feta, scallions, and parsley into a salad bowl and toss lightly.
  2. In another small bowl, mix together vinegar, mint, z'atar (if using), sugar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Slowly add olive oil, whisking to blend.
  4. Add dressing to salad and stir well.
  5. Cover and refrigerate for several hours or overnight (best), to blend flavors.
  6. Serve chilled.
