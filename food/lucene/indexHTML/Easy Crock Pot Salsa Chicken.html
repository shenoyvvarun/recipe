Easy Crock Pot Salsa Chicken

Stew,One Dish meal,Poultry,Meat,North american,Mexican,Low cholesterol,Low sat. fat,Healthy 2,Healthy,Low in...,Weeknight,Beginner cook,Time to make,Easy,Inexpensive,Equipment
Ingredients
  • boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • salsa (I use 2- 16 oz. jars)
  • corn, drained
  • black beans, drained
Method
  1. Place first four ingredients in crock pot.
  2. Cook on low 6-8 hours.
  3. 30-60 minutes prior to serving, remove chicken, shred and return to crock pot.
  4. To serve, use chicken mixture as filling inside tortillas.
  5. Add desired condiments.
