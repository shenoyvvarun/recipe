Creamy Chickpea and Tahini Casserole

Brown rice,Rice,Pasta, rice and grains,Low protein,Vegan,Low cholesterol,Low sat. fat,Healthy 2,Healthy,Low in...,Oven,Time to make,Equipment
Ingredients
  • chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • cooked brown rice
  • diced tomatoes, undrained
  • white onion, chopped
  • garlic powder (or equiv. fresh garlic)
  • dried oregano (or equiv. fresh)
  • dried basil (or equiv fresh)
  • dried parsley (or equiv. fresh)
  • tahini
  • water
  • toasted sesame seeds (optional)
Method
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Mix tahini and water until well mixed (it should get kind of"fluffy"). Set aside.
  3. Spray 9x13 pan with olive oil.
  4. Dump all ingredients into pan.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste. Mix well.
  6. Add tahini mixture, stir until well combined.
  7. Bake at 375°F for approximately 40 minutes, until top begins to brown.
  8. Sprinkle with sesame seeds, and bake 5 minutes more.
  9. Serve hot!
