Edna's Apple Crumble (Aka. Apple Crisp)

Cobblers and crisps,Apple,Fruit,Low protein,Kid Friendly,Low in...,Oven,Time to make,Equipment
Ingredients
  • granny smith apples, peeled, cored and sliced
  • packed brown sugar
  • flour
  • rolled oats
  • cinnamon
  • nutmeg
  • butter, softened
Method
  1. Put apples in an 8 or 9 inch square baking dish.
  2. Combine the rest of the ingredients in a separate bowl and mix.
  3. Spread crumble mixture over apples and bake for 35 minutes in a 375ÂºF degree oven.
  4. For an added treat top off with a little English custard.
