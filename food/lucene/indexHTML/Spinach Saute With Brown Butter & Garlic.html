Spinach Saute With Brown Butter & Garlic

Spinach,Greens,Vegetable,Low protein,Low in...,For 1 or 2,5 ingredients or less,Time to make,Easy,Number of servings
Ingredients
  • unsalted butter
  • garlic cloves, gently crushed and peeled
  • spinach, thick stems trimmed, leaves washed and dried well
  • lemon, halved
Method
  1. In a large saute pan over medium heat, melt the butter with the garlic until the butter is golden brown and smells nutty; make sure the garlic doesnâ€™t burn.
  2. Raise the heat to high and add the spinach, in batches if need be, flipping and stirring, until just barely wilted, about 1 minute Take the pan off the heat; remove the garlic.
  3. Season the spinach with salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon.
  4. Toss and serve immediately.
