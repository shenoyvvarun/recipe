Channa Masala

One Dish meal,Lunch/snacks,Chickpeas/garbanzos,Beans,Vegetable,Asian,Indian,Vegan,Spicy,Taste/mood,Stove top,Time to make,Inexpensive,Equipment
Ingredients
  • vegetable oil
  • onions (peeled and minced)
  • garlic (peeled and minced)
  • ground coriander
  • ground cumin
  • ground cayenne pepper
  • ground turmeric
  • chopped tomatoes
  • water
  • ground roasted cumin seeds
  • amchoor powder
  • paprika
  • garam masala
  • salt
  • lemon (juiced)
  • fresh, hot green chili pepper (, , minced)
  • grated fresh ginger
Method
  1. Heat oil in a large skillet.
  2. Add onions and garlic and saut√© over a medium heat until browned (3-5 minutes).
  3. Turn heat to medium-low.
  4. Add the coriander, cumin (not the roasted cumin), cayenne and turmeric.
  5. Stir for a few seconds.
  6. Add the tomatoes.
  7. Cook the tomatoes until browned lightly.
  8. Add chickpeas and a cup of water and stir.
  9. Add the roasted cumin, amchoor, paprika, garam masala, salt and lemon juice.
  10. Cook covered for 10 minutes.
  11. Remove the cover add the minced chili and ginger.
  12. Stir and cook uncovered for 30 seconds.
