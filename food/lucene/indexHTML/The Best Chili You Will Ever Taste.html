The Best Chili You Will Ever Taste

Lunch/snacks,Meat,Low cholesterol,Low sat. fat,Healthy 2,Healthy,Low in...,Spicy,Weeknight,Taste/mood,Time to make
Ingredients
  • oil
  • onions, chopped
  • garlic, minced
  • lean ground beef
  • beef sirloin, cubed
  • diced tomatoes
  • dark beer
  • strong coffee
  • tomato paste
  • beef broth
  • brown sugar
  • chili sauce
  • cumin
  • cocoa
  • oregano
  • cayenne
  • coriander
  • salt
  • kidney beans
  • chili peppers, chopped
Method
  1. Heat oil.
  2. Cook onions, garlic and meat until brown.
  3. Add tomatoes, beer, coffee, tomato paste and beef broth.
  4. Add spices Stir in 2 cans of kidney beans and peppers.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 1 1/2 hours.
  6. Add 2 remaining cans of kidney beans and simmer for another 30 minutes.
