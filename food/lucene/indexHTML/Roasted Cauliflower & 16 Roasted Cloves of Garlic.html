Roasted Cauliflower & 16 Roasted Cloves of Garlic

Cauliflower,Vegetable,North american,Low protein,Vegan,Low cholesterol,Low sat. fat,Healthy 2,Low in...,Thanksgiving,Sweet,Seasonal,Taste/mood,Roast,Oven,Served hot,3 steps or less,Time to make,Easy,Equipment,Presentation
Ingredients
  • cauliflower (trimmed and cut into bite size pieces, washed with water still on)
  • garlic cloves, peeled and lightly crushed
  • minced fresh rosemary
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • olive oil, adding more to taste
Method
  1. Mix oil, rosemary, salt, pepper and garlic together.
  2. Toss in cauliflower and place in a large casserole dish in one layer.
  3. Roast in a preheated oven at 450 degrees for 20 minutes; give a toss and bake for 10 more minutes.
