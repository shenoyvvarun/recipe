Buttered Egg Noodles (Best Ever)

Pasta, rice and grains,North american,Heirloom/historical,5 ingredients or less,Stove top,Time to make,Easy,Equipment
Ingredients
  • Wyler's chicken bouillon cubes
  • water
  • real butter
  • egg noodles
Method
  1. Bring water, 2 boullion cubes and 1/2 stick of butter to boil using med to high heat.
  2. Add egg noodles.
  3. Cook until noodles are desired texture.
  4. (About 6-8 minutes) During last minute of cooking add remaining 1/2 stick of butter.
  5. (You may add additional butter if desired. Also you may use 1 can of chicken broth instead of bullion. I suggest using bullion).
