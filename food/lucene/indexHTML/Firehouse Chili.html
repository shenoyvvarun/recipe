Firehouse Chili

Meat,Low cholesterol,Healthy 2,Low in...,Weeknight,3 steps or less,Time to make,Easy
Ingredients
  • vegetable oil
  • ground beef (for the diet concious you can also use ground turkey)
  • onion, chopped
  • fresh garlic clove, minced
  • salt
  • cayenne
  • ground cumin
  • ground oregano
  • chili powder
  • Ro-Tel tomatoes, undrained
  • tomato sauce
  • dark red kidney beans, undrained
  • tomato juice
Method
  1. Heat oil in a large pot. Brown together ground beef, onion and garlic; drain well.
  2. Add all the spices along with the Ro-tel tomatoes, tomato sauce, kidney beans and tomato juice. Mix well and bring to a boil.
  3. Simmer 1/2 hour uncovered and 1-1/2 hours covered. (If too thick you can add additional tomato juice or water).
