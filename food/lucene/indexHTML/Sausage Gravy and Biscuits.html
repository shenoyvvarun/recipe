Sausage Gravy and Biscuits

Rolls/biscuits,Breads,Breakfast,Pork,Meat,North american,Taste/mood,Oven,Stove top,Time to make,Easy,Equipment
Ingredients
  • pork sausage
  • finely-chopped onions
  • flour
  • milk
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • refrigerator biscuits
Method
  1. Cook sausage and onions in a skillet until sausage is done.
  2. Meanwhile, in a separate bowl, combine milk, Worcestershire sauce, and salt/pepper (to taste); set aside.
  3. When sausage/onion mixture is done,remove and drain excess grease.
  4. Return sausage/onion mixture back to skillet.
  5. Over medium/low heat, gradually add flour.
  6. Turn to coat sausage and cook until golden (approx).
  7. Return heat to medium.
  8. Slowly stir in milk/seasoning mixture.
  9. Cook until gravy reaches desired thickness (stirring frequently).
  10. Cook biscuits according to package directions.
  11. Serve gravy over biscuits split in half.
