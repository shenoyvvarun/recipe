The Best Pork Chop Dinner - EVER!

Pork,Potato,Vegetable,Meat,Canadian,North american,Low cholesterol,Healthy 2,Healthy,Low in...,Weeknight,Taste/mood,Time to make,Equipment
Ingredients
  • butter
  • olive oil
  • pork loin chops with bone
  • condensed cream of chicken soup, undiluted
  • mushroom, drained and chopped
  • Dijon mustard
  • chicken broth
  • garlic, minced
  • salt
  • dried basil
  • black pepper
  • potatoes, unpeeled,cut into thin slices
  • onion, sliced
Method
  1. Heat butter and oil in large skillet.
  2. Brown pork chops on both sides. Set aside.
  3. Combine soup, mushrooms, mustard, chicken broth, garlic, salt, basil and pepper in slow cooker.
  4. Add potatoes and onion, stirring to coat.
  5. Place pork chops on top of potato mixture.
  6. Cover and cook on Low 8 to 10 hours or on High 4 to 5 hours.
  7. Sprinkle with parsley.
