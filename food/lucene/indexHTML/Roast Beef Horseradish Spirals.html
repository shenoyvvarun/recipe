Roast Beef Horseradish Spirals

Spreads,Lunch/snacks,Meat,Pacific northwest,Canadian,North american,Potluck,Christmas,Thanksgiving,Weeknight,Seasonal,To go...,No Cook,Refrigerator,Beginner cook,Time to make,Easy,Equipment,Number of servings,Presentation,Technique
Ingredients
  • cream cheese, at room temperature
  • green onions, chopped about 1/2 cup
  • chopped fresh parsley
  • prepared horseradish, drained
  • salt
  • garlic powder
  • deli roast beef, sliced
  • 10-inch flour tortillas, cheese flavoured are nice
Method
  1. Combine first 6 ingredients.
  2. Place the two tortillas on work surface, spread half of cheese over each, top each with half of the roast beef slices, leaving a 1/2 inch border around edges.
  3. Roll up, wrap tightly in plastic wrap.
  4. Refrigerate until firm, 30 minutes or up to 1 day ahead.
  5. cut diagonally into 1/2 inch slices and serve.
