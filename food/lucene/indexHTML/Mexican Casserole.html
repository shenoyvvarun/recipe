Mexican Casserole

One Dish meal,Meat,North american,Mexican,Oven,Time to make,Inexpensive,Equipment
Ingredients
  • hamburger meat
  • onion, chopped
  • taco seasoning mix
  • chopped green chilies
  • enchilada sauce
  • cream of mushroom soup
  • flour tortillas
  • shredded cheddar cheese
  • taco sauce
Method
  1. Brown hamburger meat with onion and taco seasoning.
  2. Mix mushroom soup and enchilada sauce together in a bowl.
  3. Cut tortillas into triangles.
  4. In a 9x12 pan put a layer of tortillas.
  5. Then a layer of Hamburger mixture.
  6. Then a layer of Soup mixture.
  7. Then a layer of chiles.
  8. Then a layer of cheese.
  9. Then repeat again.
  10. The last layer should be tortillas.
  11. Then spread taco sauce on.
  12. Then top with cheese.
  13. Bake in a 375Â°F oven uncovered until bubbly.
