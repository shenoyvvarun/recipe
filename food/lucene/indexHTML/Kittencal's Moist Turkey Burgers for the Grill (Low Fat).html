Kittencal's Moist Turkey Burgers for the Grill (Low Fat)

Poultry,Meat,Brunch,Time to make,Easy,Equipment
Ingredients
  • ground turkey (preferably mixed white and dark meat)
  • finely shredded carrot (DO NOT squeeze out any moisture in the carrots!)
  • green onions, finely chopped (or use 1 small onion)
  • finely minced fresh garlic (about 3 cloves, or use 1-2 teaspoons garlic powder)
  • seasoning salt (can reduce to 1-1/2 teaspoons)
  • black pepper (or to taste)
  • breadcrumbs
  • eggs, slightly beaten
  • hickory-flavor barbecue sauce (can use more you may need to increase the breadcrumbs if increasing the BBQ sauce)
  • hot sauce (or to taste)
Method
  1. In a large bowl, mix together the ground turkey with all the ingredients; mix well to combine (if you find the mixture is too moist you can add in a small amount more of bread crumbs).
  2. Shape into patties, then place the patties on a plate and cover with plastic wrap; chill for 4-24 hours to blend flavors (24 hours is even better!).
  3. Place on the barbecue and grill until done (about 12-15 minutes) if made on a outdoor grill.
  4. DELICIOUS!
