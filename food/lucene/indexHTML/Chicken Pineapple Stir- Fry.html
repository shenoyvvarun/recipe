Chicken Pineapple Stir- Fry

Sauces,One Dish meal,Condiments, etc.,Chicken breast,Pineapple,Poultry,Tropical fruits,Fruit,Vegetable,Meat,Chinese,North american,Asian,Taste/mood,Stove top,Stir Fry,Time to make,Easy,Inexpensive,Equipment,Technique
Ingredients
  • soy sauce
  • sugar
  • cider vinegar
  • ketchup
  • ginger
  • minced garlic
  • cornstarch
  • boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into 2 inch strips
  • vegetable oil
  • frozen stir fry vegetables
  • pineapple chunks, drained, reserve 1/4 cup juice
Method
  1. Mix first 7 ingredients with reserved 1/4 cup pineapple juice in small bowl.
  2. In large skillet, stir-fry chicken in hot oil 5 minutes.
  3. Add vegetables, stir-fry 4 more minutes.
  4. Stir in pineapple and sauce, heat through.
  5. Serve over rice.
