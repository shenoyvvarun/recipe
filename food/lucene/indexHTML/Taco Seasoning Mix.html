Taco Seasoning Mix

Herb and spice mixes,Cooking mixes,Condiments, etc.,North american,Mexican,Free of...,Time to make,Number of servings
Ingredients
  • instant onion, Minced
  • chili powder
  • dried red pepper, Crushed
  • dried oregano
  • salt
  • cornstarch
  • instant garlic, Minced
  • cumin, Ground
Method
  1. Combine all ingredients in a small bowl and blend well.
  2. Spoon mixture onto a 6-inch square of aluminum foil and fold to make airtight. Label as Taco Seasoning Mix.
  3. Store in a cool, dry place and use within 6 months.
  4. Makes 1 package (about 2 T) of mix.
  5. Using the above recipe as a guide you can increase the amounts to make any number of packages.
  6. Taco Filling: Brown 1 lb lean ground beef in a medium skillet over medium-high heat; drain the excess grease. Add 1/2 cup water and the seasoning mix.
  7. Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Makes filling for 8 to 10 tacos.
