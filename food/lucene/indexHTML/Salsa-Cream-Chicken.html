Salsa-Cream-Chicken

One Dish meal,Chicken breast,Poultry,Meat,German,European,Very low carbs,Low in...,Spicy,Weeknight,Taste/mood,Broil/grill,5 ingredients or less,Oven,Beginner cook,Time to make,Easy,Equipment
Ingredients
  • chicken breasts
  • heavy cream
  • salsa
Method
  1. Mix heavy cream and salsa in a casserole.
  2. Wash the chicken breasts and dry them with a paper towel.
  3. Lay them into the sauce and cover them with the mixture.
  4. Bake 1 hour at 400Â° F or until chicken is tender and cooked through.
  5. If you don't eat meat you can substitute the chicken with feta and bake it for approx 20 minutes.
  6. Serve with white wine, pita bread, rice or/ and fresh garden salad.
