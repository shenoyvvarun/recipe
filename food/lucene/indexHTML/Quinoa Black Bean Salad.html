Quinoa Black Bean Salad

Grains,Black beans,Beans,Pasta, rice and grains,Vegan,Low cholesterol,Low sat. fat,Healthy 2,Healthy,Low in...,Served cold,Time to make,Presentation
Ingredients
  • quinoa
  • water
  • olive oil
  • fresh lime juice
  • cumin
  • coriander
  • fresh minced cilantro
  • minced scallions
  • black beans, rinsed and drained
  • diced tomatoes
  • diced bell pepper
  • minced jalapeno chiles
Method
  1. Cook the quinoa in the water. Allow to cool slightly.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the oil, lime juice, cumin, coriander, cilantro and scallions.
  3. Stir in the beans, tomatoes, bell peppers and chilies.
  4. Add the cooled quinoa.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve cold.
