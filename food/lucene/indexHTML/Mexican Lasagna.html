Mexican Lasagna

Poultry,Meat,Tex Mex,Southwestern u.s.,North american,Mexican,Oven,Time to make,Easy,Equipment
Ingredients
  • lean ground turkey
  • taco seasoning
  • chopped onion
  • tomato sauce (low sodium)
  • light cottage cheese
  • egg substitute
  • corn tortillas
  • light monterey jack cheese, shredded
  • light cheddar cheese, shredded
Method
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Brown turkey, drain (if necessary).
  3. Add seasoning packet, onion, tomato sauce and salsa.
  4. Simmer 5-7 minutes.
  5. Combine cottage cheese and egg in small bowl.
  6. Spray 9 x 13 pan with cooking spray.
  7. Assemble lasagna in the following order: 1/2 meat mixture, 1/2 tortillas, 1/2 cottage cheese mixture, 1/2 cheese, repeat.
  8. Bake at 350°F for 25 minutes.
  9. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
