Kittencal's Sausage Sawmill Gravy (With Biscuits)

Lunch/snacks,Pork,Eggs/dairy,Meat,Brunch,Taste/mood,Beginner cook,Stove top,Time to make,Easy,Inexpensive,Equipment
Ingredients
  • bulk pork sausage (can use sausages with casings removed, or turkey sausages)
  • onion, chopped (optional)
  • crushed red pepper flakes (or to taste, adjust to suit heat level)
  • all-purpose flour, plus
  • all-purpose flour (for a thicker gravy increase slightly)
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • fresh ground black pepper (or to taste)
  • prepared biscuits (optional)
Method
  1. Cook the sausage meat with onion (if using) in a large skillet over medium heat until sausage meat no longer pink; drain fat.
  2. Add in crushed red pepper flakes and cook stirring until the meat is lightly browned (about 12-15 minutes, the meat must be browned).
  3. Stir in 1/4 cup plus 2 teaspoon flour into the skillet and mix until dissolved with the meat (about 2 minutes).
  4. Add in the cream and Worcestershire sauce; cook over medium-low heat until thickened and bubbly (about 5 minutes).
  5. Add in salt and ground black pepper.
  6. Serve over biscuits.
