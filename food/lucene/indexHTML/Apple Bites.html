Apple Bites

Breakfast,Lunch/snacks,Apple,Fruit,Low protein,Low cholesterol,Low sat. fat,Healthy 2,Toddler Friendly,Healthy,Kid Friendly,Low in...,Brunch,To go...,For 1 or 2,5 ingredients or less,No Cook,Beginner cook,Time to make,Easy,Inexpensive,Number of servings,Presentation,Technique
Ingredients
  • apple (which ever kind you prefer)
  • cinnamon (or more/less - how you prefer.)
Method
  1. Cut up apple (with or without skin - your choice) into bite sized chunks.
  2. Put the chopped apple into a container with a lid (ex. an empty margarine container).
  3. Sprinkle on the cinnamon, put the lid on the container, and gently shake so cinnamon covers apple.
  4. Eat and enjoy immediately.
