Easy Black Beans and Rice

One Dish meal,White rice,Rice,Black beans,Onions,Peppers,Beans,Vegetable,Pasta, rice and grains,Southwestern u.s.,North american,Vegan,Potluck,Spicy,Taste/mood,To go...,Stove top,Served hot,Time to make,Equipment,Presentation
Ingredients
  • olive oil
  • onion, chopped finely
  • red pepper, diced
  • garlic, minced
  • uncooked white rice
  • vegetable broth, see my recipe posted or use store bought
  • ground cumin
  • curry powder
  • cayenne pepper
  • canned black beans, drained
Method
  1. In a stockpot over medium-high heat, heat the oil.
  2. Add the onion,pepper and garlic and saute for 4 minutes.
  3. Add the rice and saute for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the vegetable broth, bring to a boil, cover and lower the heat and cook for 20 minutes.
  5. Add the spices and black beans.
