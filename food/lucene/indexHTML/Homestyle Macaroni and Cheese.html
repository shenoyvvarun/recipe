Homestyle Macaroni and Cheese

Elbow macaroni,Cheese,Eggs/dairy,Pasta, rice and grains,Kosher,Taste/mood,Oven,Time to make,Inexpensive,Equipment
Ingredients
  • macaroni
  • butter
  • flour
  • 1% low-fat milk
  • low-fat cream cheese, softened
  • salt
  • fresh ground black pepper
  • Dijon mustard
  • sharp cheddar cheese
  • fresh breadcrumb
  • butter, melted
  • chopped fresh parsley
Method
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Cook macaroni (1/2 pound= 2 cups dry) until just al dente.
  3. You can use elbows for a traditional look, but try scoobi-doo-- it works well in this recipe.
  4. Drain pasta well.
  5. While the mac is boiling, melt the 1/4-cup butter in a large pot.
  6. Whisk in the flour.
  7. Over med heat, whisk until smooth and bubbly (only about a minute).
  8. Stir in milk, cream cheese, salt, pepper& mustard.
  9. Keep cooking and stirring until sauce is thickened-- about three or four minutes.
  10. Cut the block of cheddar into half-inch cubes.
  11. Add drained macaroni and cubed cheddar& combine well.
  12. Pour into a casserole sprayed with Pam.
  13. In a small bowl, combine the remaining ingredients and sprinkle over top.
  14. Dust with paprika if desired.
  15. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until top is golden brown and casserole is heated through.
