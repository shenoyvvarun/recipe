Kittencal's Brown Sugar Caramel Sauce or Ice Cream Topping

Sweet sauces,Sauces,Condiments, etc.,Low protein,Low in...,5 ingredients or less,Time to make,Easy,Number of servings
Ingredients
  • salted butter (no substitutions)
  • salt
  • brown sugar (well packed)
  • heavy whipping cream, unwhipped (35% cream, no less! can use a little more cream)
  • vanilla extract (optional)
Method
  1. In a small saucepan melt butter with salt.
  2. Add the brown sugar; whisk until combined and thickened (about 2 minutes).
  3. Whisk in the whipping cream, until thoroughly blended (2 more minutes).
  4. Mix in vanilla (if using) until combined.
  5. NOTE; this sauce will thicken up more when refrigerated.
