Kittencal's Tuna Melt Sandwich

Lunch/snacks,Tuna,Saltwater fish,Vegetable,Kid Friendly,Brunch,Taste/mood,Broil/grill,Oven,Beginner cook,Served hot,Time to make,Easy,Equipment,Presentation
Ingredients
  • solid white tuna packed in water, well drained
  • low-fat mayonnaise (can use more, or use regular-fat mayonnaise)
  • fresh lemon juice (or use 1 teaspoon red wine vinegar)
  • celery rib, finely chopped
  • green onions, finely chopped
  • fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • tomatoes
  • swiss cheese (or use as much cheese as you like, or use mozzarella cheese)
Method
  1. Set oven to broiler heat.
  2. In a bowl mix tuna, mayo, lemon juice (or red wine vinegar) celery, green onions and parsley; mix well.
  3. Add in seasoned salt and lemon pepper to taste.
  4. Place the bread slices on a baking sheet and broil for 1 minute or until lightly toasted.
  5. Remove from heat, divide and spread the tuna mixture on top of each bread slice.
  6. Place one cheese slice over the tuna, then one tomato slice (you can use 2 tomato slices if your wish) then another slice of cheese on top.
  7. Return the layered bread to oven and broil for about 3 minutes, or until the cheese is melted.
