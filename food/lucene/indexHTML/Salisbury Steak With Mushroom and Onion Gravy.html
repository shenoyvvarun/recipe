Salisbury Steak With Mushroom and Onion Gravy

Meat,Low in...,For 1 or 2,Time to make,Easy,Number of servings
Ingredients
  • ground beef
  • finely chopped onion
  • cracker crumb
  • egg, slightly beaten
  • prepared horseradish
  • salt
  • pepper
  • butter
  • onion, sliced, into, rings
  • fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • butter
  • flour
  • cream
  • chicken broth
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • hot sauce
Method
  1. Combine ground beef, chopped onions, cracker crumbs, egg, horseradish, salt and pepper. Shape into patties.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons butter in skillet.
  3. Cook patties till no longer pink inside - approx 8 minutes each side.
  4. In separate pan, melt 3 tablespoons butter. Saut√© onion rings and mushrooms til tender.
  5. Remove patties from pan.
  6. Stir flour into pan, scraping bottom of pan. Add dash of salt and pepper. Cook 2 minutes. Stir in cream to form a thick paste.
  7. Slowly stir in chicken broth - stir till thick.
  8. Add a few dashes of Worcestershire sauce and hot sauce. Stir till mixed well.
  9. Simmer 5 minutes Add mushrooms, onions and patties.
  10. Heat 10 minutes.
