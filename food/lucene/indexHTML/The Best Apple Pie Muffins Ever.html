The Best Apple Pie Muffins Ever

Muffins,Quick breads,Breads,Breakfast,Lunch/snacks,Apple,Fruit,Sweet,Brunch,Taste/mood,To go...,For large groups,Oven,Time to make,Equipment,Number of servings
Ingredients
  • firmly packed brown sugar
  • all-purpose flour
  • unsalted butter, melted
  • cinnamon
  • firmly packed brown sugar
  • vegetable oil
  • egg
  • vanilla
  • all-purpose flour
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • buttermilk
  • diced peeled firm tart apples (such as Spy, Granny Smith)
Method
  1. Note:.
  2. cinnamon could be added to the batter ( about 1 teaspoon or less).
  3. Topping: In a small bowl toss together sugar, flour, butter and cinnamon until crumbly; set aside.
  4. Muffins: In a large bowl whisk together brown sugar, oil, egg and vanilla until smooth.
  5. In a separate bowl, sift together flour, soda and salt.
  6. Stir oil mixture into flour mixture alternately with buttermilk.
  7. Fold in apples, mixing just until combined.
  8. Spoon into greased muffin cups filling 3/4 full.
  9. Sprinkle topping over evenly.
  10. Bake at 350Â°F for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown and tops spring back.
