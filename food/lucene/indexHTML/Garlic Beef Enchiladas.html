Garlic Beef Enchiladas

Poultry,Meat,North american,Mexican,Weeknight,Oven,Time to make,Inexpensive,Equipment
Ingredients
  • onion, chopped
  • flour
  • chili powder
  • salt
  • garlic powder
  • ground cumin
  • ground sage
  • stewed tomatoes
  • garlic, minced

  • tomato sauce
  • chili powder
  • cumin

  • salt
  • garlic powder
  • flour tortillas
Method
  1. In saucepan, cook beef or turkey and onion until browned; drain.
  2. Add flour and seasonings.
  3. Mix well.
  4. Stir in tomatoes and bring to boil.
  5. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 15 minutes.
  6. In another saucepan, saute garlic in butter.
  7. Stir in flour until blended.
  8. Gradually stir in broth and bring to boil.
  9. Cook and stir for 2 minutes until bubbly.
  10. Stir in tomato sauce and seasonings and heat through.
  11. Pour 1 1/2 cup sauce in 13x9 inch pan.
  12. Spread about 1/4 cup beef mixture down center of tortilla and top with 1-2 Tbsp cheese.
  13. Roll up tightly and place seam side-down over sauce in pan.
  14. Repeat with remaining tortillas and top all with remaining sauce.
  15. Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes.
  16. Sprinkle with remaining cheese and bake uncovered for 10-15 minutes until cheese melts.
