Creamy Macaroni Salad

Elbow macaroni,Vegetable,Pasta, rice and grains,Potluck,Camping,To go...,For large groups,Refrigerator,Served cold,Time to make,Equipment,Number of servings,Presentation
Ingredients
  • large elbow macaroni, cooked to desired tenderness
  • thinly sliced celery
  • finely diced sweet onion
  • red sweet bell pepper, finely diced
  • sliced pimento stuffed olive
  • prepared yellow mustard
  • apple cider vinegar
  • sweet pickle relish
  • sugar
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • celery seed
  • bacon, prepared as in Homemade Fresh Bacon Bits
Method
  1. Mix cooled macaroni, celery, onions, bell pepper and green olives together in a large bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl mix together the mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, pickle relish, sugar, salt, pepper and celery seed, stirring well.
  3. Stir dressing into the macaroni mixture.
  4. Stir in the crumbled bacon.
  5. Serve chilled.
  6. For another variation, omit the green olives, add sliced black olives and a container of grape tomatoes.
  7. For a meal type salad, add diced cooked chicken or tuna fish.
