Cluster all food into 2 classes based on ingredients:
         
Look at the Circle packing graph of all food, which was obtained by clustering food based on ingredients. The fast kmeans algorithm is used to perform the clustering. Only the prominent 100 ingredients in the entire recipe set was used.


Cluster all food into 2 classes based on associated tags:
         
Look at the Circle packing graph of all food, which was obtained by clustering food based on tags which are associated with each recipe. Kmeans was used and around 20 tags was used for this clustering operation.
